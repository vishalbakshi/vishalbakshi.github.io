About Me

I currently reside in the Bay Area, but I grew up in the pleasant plains of Iowa. I'm an Adjunct Faculty in the Engineering & Technology Department at the City College of San Francisco, and an Academic Tutor at One Smart Kid focusing on STEM subjects and Standardized Test Prep for students in grades 4 through 12.

Before that, I taught high school STEM courses at the Maharishi School of the Age of Enlightenment after teaching 9th grade Algebra at the Detroit Collegiate Preparatory High School.

You can contact me at vbakshi@ccsf.edu or vdbakshi@gmail.com.
