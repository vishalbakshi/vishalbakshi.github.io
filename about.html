About Me

I currently reside in the Bay Area, but I grew up in the pleasant plains of Iowa. I'm an Adjunct Faculty in the Engineering & Technology Department at the City College of San Francisco, and a Student Data Champion (i.e. Educational Data Consultant) at Measure Education.

Before that, I taught high school STEM courses at the Maharishi School of the Age of Enlightenment after teaching 9th grade Algebra in the Detroit Public Schools Community District.

I studied Civil and Mechanical Enginering at Iowa State University, and Structural Engineering at the University of Illinois.

You can contact me at vbakshi@ccsf.edu or vdbakshi@gmail.com.
