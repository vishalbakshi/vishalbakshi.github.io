Language is a tool by which culture is reinforced. We are trained in the language of homophobia and thus taught that outside of family, love is synonymous with romance and sexual attraction. The family unit is the smallest unit of community and our language does not allow love to flow freely outside of it. Think about the last time you told a friend you loved them---did you say love you or claim that phrase with the all important I? Personally, I have never said i love you to a friend unless I considered them family, and even then, when I say it to an adult woman, I stress over a potential misunderstanding. bell hooks defines love as action that combines care, affection, recognition, knowledge, respect, responsibility, trust, commitment and honest communication. I cannot think of a relationship in any setting where these characteristics would be detrimental, yet we avoid the word love to avoid coming off as too sentimental. Every harm experienced in our imperialist-white supremacist-capitalist-patriarchal society can be traced back to a lack of love. The irresponsibility of our federal government when handling the COVID-19 outbreak. The lack of respect in congress for women's reproductive rights. Voter suppression. Mass media gaslighting. Domestic violence. Locomotion to all psychological regions characterized by loving action is blocked by the cultural embarrassment around love. We have the power to make new connections and forge new paths.
